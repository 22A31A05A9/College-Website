About the Department of Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Pragati Engineering College is a premier institution for computer science education and research. Our department is committed to providing a world-class education to our students, and our faculty are dedicated to advancing the field of computer science through innovative research.

Our department offers a range of undergraduate and graduate degree programs in computer science, including Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech), Master of Technology (M.Tech), and Ph.D. programs. Our curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science and engineering, as well as the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in a rapidly changing field.

Mission

DM1: To produce competent software professionals.
DM2: To induce application-oriented and research capabilities in students for the betterment of society.
DM3: To inculcate ethics and human values in students to adapt to the dynamism in the field of computing technology.

Vision

To emerge as a center of technical expertise in the field of computer science and engineering by producing globally competent professionals with technical & research capabilities, ethical values, and team spirit.
